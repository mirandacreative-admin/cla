Career Opportunities

CLA Engineers, Inc. is a well-established civil engineering firm that serves a diverse clientele that includes towns, utilities, developers, property owners, schools, realtors, architects, and attorneys. We are committed to maintaining the open culture and work/family life balance that characterizes the company. Our employees are professional and knowledgeable, and we are committed to providing superior service to our clients. Our services include surveying, civil and structural engineering, environmental permitting, and construction administration and inspection.

Our mission is to continue to increase our flexibility and our capability to serve a diverse clientele, and with that goes a commitment to the professional growth and development of our employees. If you want to work in a professional and open environment where your professional development is encouraged and supported, give us a call.

Please check back frequently for future employment opportunities with CLA Engineers.
