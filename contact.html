Directions

From I-95/I-395 (North bound):

Take I-395 N exit (Exit #76), (a left lane exit) to NORWICH/PLAINFIELD
Take exit 79A (Route 2A) which takes you to Route 12 ( a T- intersection);
At stoplight, take left onto Route 12 (north) - this will take you into Norwich;
When you get to your 3rd traffic light you will want to continue on straight. This takes you over the Shetucket River.
Take a left at the 4th traffic light (Main Street);
Our office is on the left hand side, 317 Main Street, approx 2 blocks down. It's a 2 story brick building. You can park on Main Street or down Fuller Court (a side street). Down in back of our building is additional parking.

From Route 2 (Eastbound from Hartford/Colchester);

Route 2 ends at Washington Street (Backus Hospital); Turn Right onto Washington Street;
Stay on Washington St. (bear right) and you will reach the Norwich Harbor /marina area; Counting the Dunkin Donuts stoplight (on left near approach to harbor area), go through 3 stop lights; at the 4th stoplight turn right onto Main Street.
