Services

CLA Engineers, Inc. is a consulting engineering firm specializing in all aspects of Civil Engineering and Construction. Incorporated in 1984, our office is located at 317 Main Street in downtown Norwich.

Our composition of personnel enables us to take a project from the conceptual and planning stages through the design and finished construction project. We are able to assist our private clients in understanding the regulations of Local, State & Federal agencies in the planning phase in order to more efficiently design the project and avoid costly delays.

CLA Engineers, Inc. is a corporate member of the U.S. Green building Council, founder and administrator of the LEED Green Building Rating System.

For municipal or private clients utilizing State or Federal funding, we can provide grant application and administration services, including all cost accountability necessary for reimbursements.

Upon completion of the design, we are able to assist the client in acquiring bids, and evaluating and making recommendations for award. We then provide detailed inspection services and construction administration during construction.

Due to our project team approach, we are able to provide our clients with all the necessary services to take a project from conception through construction. This capability and experience provides professional and efficient service to the client.
