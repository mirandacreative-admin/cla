Town Services

CLA Engineers has provided on-call services for several municipalities within the Eastern Connecticut region. Acting as Town Engineers to Selectmen, Boards, Commissions, Public Works Departments, Recreation Departments, and School Departments, CLA Engineers has provided a wide range of services including the following:

  • Technical support of town facilities
  • Review of subdivisions
  • Review of commercial development
  • Review of bond estimates
  • Periodic or Full time Construction inspections
  • Environmental assessments
  • Updating technical regulations
  • Sidewalks / Streetscapes
  • Transfer Station / Landfill Permitting
  • Recreation Projects
  • Public Works Garages / Salt Storage Facilities
  • Structural Assessments
  • Retaining Walls
  • Road and drainage reconstruction Projects
  • Bridge Reconstruction
  • Stormwater permitting
  • Land Surveying
  • Feasibility studies

In addition to the above services, CLA Engineers also provides on-call services to municipally owned utilities. These services include the following:

  • Planning, design and inspection of water and sanitary sewer extension projects
  • Updating WPCA technical regulations
  • Updating service area mapping
  • Review of public and private sewer and water extensions
  • Review of bond estimates
  • Periodic or Full time Construction inspections
  • Call-Before-You-Dig mark-out services

CLA Engineers also has assisted many municipal clients with grant and loan applications to various Federal and State agencies. We have experience in the following grant/loan programs:

  • Community Development Block Grants
  • USDA – Rural Development Grant/Loans
  • Small Town Economic Assistance Program (STEAP) grants
  • Clean Water Fund (CWF) Grants / Loans
  • CT DOT Local Bridge Program
  • Department of Economic and Community Development (DECD) Grants
  • ISTEA/TEA-21 Grants
  • CT Department of Housing
  • Local Public Works Program Grants
