Bridge Engineering

CLA Engineers provides a full range of engineering services for bridge rehabilitation and construction.  Our team provides the breadth of experience necessary to facilitate bridge construction projects, including:

  • Structural engineering
  • Foundation design
  • Wetlands delineation and permitting
  • U.S. Army Corps of Engineers permitting
  • Roadway design
  • Drainage design
  • Surveying
  • Construction Inspection

Our bridge design experience includes private bridges, pedestrian bridges, local roadway, and state roadway bridges. We have assisted municipalities and other clients with the Connecticut local bridge program and have provided support for acquiring funding grants for bridge construction.
