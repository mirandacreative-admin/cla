Water & Sewer

CLA has provided utility engineering services to municipal and private clients since 1984. Our team of in-house professionals have the specialized skills and experience needed to develop your utility project from concept to completion, on schedule and within budget. In addition to our engineering services, we offer construction inspection of our utility projects with experienced, full-time or part-time construction inspectors.
